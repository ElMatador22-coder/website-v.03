Questions

Fair questions, straight answers.

What is a "System of Action"?

A system of record stores what happened. A system of insight tells you what it means. A system of action closes the loop: it detects the signal, decides the play, and executes the work — drafts, sequences, scheduling, CRM updates — with you approving instead of typing.

Does SalesOgre replace my CRM?

It replaces the time you spend feeding it. SalesOgre keeps your system of record updated automatically while the platform does the selling work. Your admin gets their reports; you get your week back.

Why isn't SalesOgre agentic?

Because for 99% of sellers, autonomy is the wrong product today. You can't unsend an agent's mistake to an Economic Buyer, buyers actively avoid automated outreach, and selling is a judgment job. SalesOgre does the work — reads, drafts, plans, updates — and stages it for your approval. Autonomy comes later, on the Enterprise Platform, only where you've earned trust in it.

Is Coach really free?

Yes. Free, no credit card, runs in your browser. It's the on-ramp — we'd rather prove it than pitch it.

How does the coaching stay current?

Every note you add to a meeting triggers a recalibration: stakeholder reads update, risks re-score, and the next best action re-plans. The coaching tracks the deal you have today, not the one from last week's pipeline review.
